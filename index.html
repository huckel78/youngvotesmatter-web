The Roadmap

ROAD TO NOVEMBER 2026

Four phases from foundation to full national launch — built on Lean Startup methodology.

01
Months 1–3

Foundation

  • File 501(c)(4) + 501(c)(3) entities
  • Submit foundation grant applications
  • Integrate LegiScan API
  • Build ZIP-to-rep MVP
  • Pre-produce 30 content pieces
02
Months 4–6

Pilot

  • 2 pilot campuses & 30 ambassadors
  • Federal tracker live (535 members)
  • 5 priority states: TX, FL, GA, AZ, PA
  • Call Your Rep module live
  • Target: 2,500 registrations
03
Months 7–12

Scale

  • 50+ campuses, 20+ states
  • 150+ ambassadors nationally
  • All 50 states bill tracker live
  • Full congressional scorecard
  • Target: 100,000+ registrations
04
November 2026

Go-Live

  • All features across 3 domains
  • Full 50-state scorecard published
  • 100-campus GOTV push
  • National press launch
  • Target: 150,000+ registrations
Legal Architecture

DUAL ENTITY STRUCTURE

Modeled after the ACLU — maximum advocacy power plus full grant eligibility.

To protect American democracy effectively, we operate through two distinct legal branches to fight in the courts, lobby in legislatures, and educate in classrooms simultaneously.
