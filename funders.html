Financial Transparency

OUR FUNDERS

Democracy requires radical transparency. See exactly how our operations are funded.

100% Independent. 100% Transparent.

Project OMega operates independently of any political party or corporate PAC. Our funding comes from a coalition of philanthropic foundations, grassroots donors, and pro-democracy advocates who believe in the power of the youth vote.

OMega Education Fund [501(c)(3)]

Supported by tax-deductible contributions. This funding is strictly restricted to non-partisan voter registration, civic education, and our campus ambassador programs.

OMega Civic Action [501(c)(4)]

Supported by non-deductible contributions. This allows us to actively lobby for pro-voter legislation and hold specific elected officials accountable for suppressing the youth vote.

Major Institutional Partners

A full disclosure report of our 2026 institutional partners will be published here prior to our Go-Live date. We pledge to disclose all donors contributing over $5,000 annually.
